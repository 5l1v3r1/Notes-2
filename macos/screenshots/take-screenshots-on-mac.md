# Take Screenshots on Mac

#### Take a Screenshot of Whole Screen

* Command + Shift + 3

#### Take a Screenshot of Part of Screen

* Command + Shift + 4
* Drag to select an area.      

#### Take a Screenshot of a Window

* Command + Shift + 4
* Press space bar.
